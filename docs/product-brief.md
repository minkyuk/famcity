# FamCity — Product Brief

## What Is It?

FamCity is a private social feed for families. Think of it as a Twitter/Instagram hybrid that lives behind a single shared link — no app store, no signup friction, just open and post. Family members can share moments, funny videos, voice messages, and photos in one scrollable feed that everyone can react to and comment on.

---

## Who Is It For?

A single family group. Not the public. Not friends. Just family. The URL is the password (for now).

---

## Core User Stories

### Posting

| As a family member, I want to... | So that... |
|----------------------------------|-----------|
| Post a text update               | I can share news or thoughts quickly |
| Paste a YouTube link and have it embed | I can share a video I found |
| Upload a photo from my phone or computer | I can share a moment visually |
| Record my voice and post it | I can send a voice message to everyone |
| See who posted what and when | I know who shared the content |

### Engaging

| As a family member, I want to... | So that... |
|----------------------------------|-----------|
| React with an emoji to any post  | I can respond without typing |
| Leave a comment on a post        | I can have a conversation |
| See all reactions on a post      | I feel the group's energy |

### Browsing

| As a family member, I want to... | So that... |
|----------------------------------|-----------|
| See all posts in reverse chronological order | I catch up on what I missed |
| Open a post to see full comments | I can read the whole conversation |
| View an image full-screen        | I can see the photo clearly |

---

## Non-Goals (Phase 1)

- No user accounts or login
- No direct messages
- No notifications
- No video upload (YouTube links only)
- No moderation tools
- No public sharing

---

## Success Criteria for MVP

- [ ] Any family member can post in under 30 seconds
- [ ] All post types (text, image, YouTube, audio) work on mobile Safari and Chrome
- [ ] The feed loads in under 2 seconds
- [ ] Reactions and comments work without page reload
- [ ] The app is deployed and accessible via a shared URL

---

## Design Principles

1. **Frictionless** — No signup, no tutorial. Open and post.
2. **Mobile-first** — Most family members will use phones.
3. **Warm** — UI should feel personal and inviting, not corporate.
4. **Reliable** — If grandma posts, it must show up. No silent failures.
